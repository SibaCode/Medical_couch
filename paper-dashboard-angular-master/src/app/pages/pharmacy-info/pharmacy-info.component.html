Our Offered Services

Medical Couch is a website that caters for patients, doctors and pharmacies. It is designed to make patient-doctor consultations easier through online bookings. Medical Couch enables doctors to upload patient’s prescription at nearby pharmacies in accordance to the patient’s preferred pick up date and time.

Helps pharmacies not to only have an organised online prescription upload system which helps to make hand out easy, but to be advertised to the new platforms of social media.

Organises prescription for pharmacies by preparing a list with patients information to help pack beforehand.

Makes prescription hand outs much faster by providing patients information to enter on the pharmacy system

It provides a pack-handout system without any delays
