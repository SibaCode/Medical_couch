About Us

Medical Couch is a website that caters for patients, doctors and pharmacies. It is designed to make patient-doctor consultations easier through online bookings.

Medical Couch enables doctors to upload patient’s prescription at nearby pharmacies in accordance to the patient’s preferred pick up date and time.

Mission

Medical Couch intends to build a relationship amongst patients, doctor and pharmacies through a user friendly, reliable and innovative system.

Vision

To be the first choice booking website to patients, doctors and pharmacies in South Africa and extend globally.

Goal

To achieve loyalty from all three parties; patients, doctors and pharmacies To be the number 1 solution to online booking and online prescription uploads.

Services

With patients being doctors and pharmacies’ main focus, they need to be kept happy and their demands met and Medical Couch has the solution to that.

Medical Couch helps doctors organize their daily schedule through online calendars

Helps patients to make online bookings by giving them the nearest doctors availability schedule.

It filters out the doctors, giving patients the nearest doctors that cater for their medical aid network.

It allows patients to skip queues and receive their prescription and receive your prescription already packed at the nearby pharmacy in accordance to the patients preferred collection date and time.

It provides a pack-handout system without any delays.

Helps doctors to connect with their patients even before consultations.

Advertises the doctors practice and reduces admin work for the practice.

Advertise the pharmacy.

Organises prescription for pharmacies by preparing a list with patients information to help pack beforehand.

Makes prescription hand outs much faster by providing patients information to enter on the pharmacy system.
