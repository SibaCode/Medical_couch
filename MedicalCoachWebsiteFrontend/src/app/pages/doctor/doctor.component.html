Medical Couch is a website that caters for patients, doctors and pharmacies. It is designed to make patient-doctor consultations easier through online bookings. Medical Couch enables doctors to upload patient’s prescription at nearby pharmacies in accordance to the patient’s preferred pick up date and time.

Helps doctors organize their daily schedule through online calendars.

Helps doctors to connect with their patients even before consultations.

Advertises the doctors practice.

Reduces admin work for the practice
